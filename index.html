About me

Welcome to my portfolio page. I am a freelance data scientist/analyst and python developer. On the page below you will find some personal recommendations from former colleagues of mine as well as some links to some projects that I am proud of (see "Selected Work"). Please have a look and if you are interested in contacting me further please do so with the information provided at the very bottom of the page. I am currently looking for a new full time role so I look forward to hearing from you!

A little more about me: I have a long history working with data. I graduated in 2020 from the University of Utah with a PhD in physics. Immediatly thereafter I worked as a Data Scientist for Western Governors University and roughly a year after that as a Data Scientist for PayPal. After my time with PayPal I decided To take some time for personal projects as well as continuing to work as a freelance data scientist and an AI consultant.

One of my current, new, interests is using LLMs for fullscale software development and data engineering. As a testbed for this new direction I have been building my own peer-to-peer marketplace website. Using ChatGPT-3.5 as the workhorse developer, I am free to do the systems architecture of the site. This is a very interesting new direction in the industry and I am excited to see where it goes. I believe it may lead to developers being able to create new, innovative startups with the fraction of traditional seed funding.
