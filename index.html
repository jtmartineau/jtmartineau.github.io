About me

I am passionate about data science, machine learning and software development. On this site you will find examples of my original work. My interests run broad and deep. I thrive on finding new patterns in data, inventing new tools and technology, and making new and exciting digital art. I believe that scientific inquriy and technological progress - while not inherently good or bad in themselves - are the only reliable tools humanity has to improve itself. Stewarding the massive power they possess toward moral outcomes is the job of every contientious citizen.

I graduated in 2019 with a PhD in physics. Immediatly thereafter I worked as a Data Scientist I for Western Governors University and roughly a year after that as a Data Scientist II for PayPal. After my time at PayPal I took some time to start my own game development company Taalon Games LLC. and to develop my first game, Helrunner.

Presently, as of October 2023, I am looking for a new role. I have learned over the past couple of years that doing meaningful work is more important to me than earning a lot of money. If you are looking for a kind, innovative, versatile, hard working team member I would be very happy to set up an interview. Thank you for the visit, I hope to hear from you soon!
